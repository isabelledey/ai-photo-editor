# Accessibility Statement

AI Face Stylizer is committed to accessibility and aims to conform to WCAG 2.1 AA principles.

## Measures Implemented
- Semantic page structure and consistent heading hierarchy.
- Keyboard-accessible controls for upload, detection, enhancement, and admin tools.
- Visible focus states and clear interaction targets.
- Sufficient color contrast in dark-mode UI.
- Text alternatives for images and icon-only controls.
- Status messaging for processing and errors.

## Known Limitations
- Third-party widgets or model outputs may not always be fully accessible.
- Performance on older browsers or assistive technology stacks may vary.

## Ongoing Improvements
We continuously test and improve accessibility. If you encounter a barrier, please report it with steps to reproduce, browser/device details, and assistive technology used.
